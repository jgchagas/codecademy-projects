Going to a Fashion Show

Fashion Weeks can be both amazingly fun and incredibly overwhelming, especially if you are a novice. Lucky for you, darling, I am here to give an insider's guide and make your debut a pleasurable experience. By taking my tips and tricks, and following your gut, you MUST HAVE an unforgettable experience!

If you are lucky or connected you can get an invite, sans the price tag, darling. But if you don't have those superpowers, I can help you out. First, plan out which shows are most important to you and make a schedule. And this is a MUST HAVE: a budget. If you are worryied about blowing your cash the whole time you will not be able to enjoy yourself.hThen check out prices, days, and times, and prioritize the designers you want to see most. Lastly, purchase your tickets and get excited!

Always be true to your own sense of style, unless it requires a cape! You don't want to be uncomfortable the whole time and it will show. Remember, fashion is about expressing yourself! It is also important to wear shoes that will be comfortable all day long. Obviously you want to look good, but consider you will be on your feet all day long, so be prepared.
